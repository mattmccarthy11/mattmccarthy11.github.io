Summary

I am a contrarian who has a passion for innovation in all areas in life. I believe that through the power of technology we can empower the disadvantaged to have an equal playing field. I find it is only inevitable that as the internet continues to spread to the developing world. We will see a global market of small local entrepreneurs selling their craft or skill to the global community. My life mission is to accelerate this change through aspects such as drones, and widespread affordable internet education. I envision a rural village gains access to internet. Then can rent a drone to pick up their good and from there it can be distributed globally. All my businesses do is fundamentally serve as a tool to leverage to achieve this vision.

I am currently exploring the possibilities of IoT applications in the corporate world with a focus to reduce a company's global carbon footprint. Also exploring the interaction of Virtual Reality and Real Time data Analytics and Machine Learning/AI to provide a fully immersive platform to analyze and perform data operations.

I have a drive of mastery of skills such as: Programming and Meditation Although I am gifted with hyperactivity and the wonderful brainstorming abilities it gives you. Meditation is something I struggle with. When i do achieve meditation i find clarity and access to raw brain power unlike any. So I'm constantly working to achieve this state.

I wish to travel the world to meet, interact and grow from experiencing new cultures and ideas.

In 2012 After a local hockey player Jack Jablonski suffered from a spinal cord injury and having a father who was paralyzed and became a quadriplegic when I was just 3. I understood both the family and financial burdens their family would encounter in the future. I was determined to raise money for their foundation. I set up a clothing company with his logo and used my skills to build a line for their family. Our team sold shirts at 100s of arenas around the globe.
